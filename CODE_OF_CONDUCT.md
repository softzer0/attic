# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

Be considerate, assume good intent, give actionable technical feedback, and respect differing experience levels and viewpoints. Harassment, discrimination, threats, publishing private information, and sustained disruptive behavior are not acceptable.

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate these expectations, and may temporarily or permanently ban repeat offenders. Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.
